Waves at Matsushima ditigally recreated

Coordinate Landscape

This is my digital landscape adapation of Waves at Matsushima
painted by Tawaraya Sotatsu. This being one of my favorite paintings,
the process of recreating it in a medium that I feel strips it
of its expressive quality, was hard. However, I feel that the piece
I made is also expressive, but has lost the original's feeling of longing
and isolation.
